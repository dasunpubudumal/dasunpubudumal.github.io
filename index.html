Record Matching for Hotel Data

If you are a hotelier, your primary focus is sales. For your sales to expand, you can register your hotel in an agency system like Expedia or HotelBeds. These agency systems are integrated with tour operator systems which are used by travel agents to book hotels for their customers. This article discusses the record matching process of how the de-duplication occur when multiple agency systems are integrated with aforementioned tour operators. The article was published in the popular travel journal Travolution, and the link is attached here.

Demystifying Shazam

This is probably the most popular article I've written. It was published in The Startup, a popular publication in Medium. This article discusses the algorithm behind the popular music recognition app Shazam, and it refers to the original paper published by the Shazam authors themselves.
